REGISTRATION INSTRUCTIONS
TESOL INTERNATIONAL CERTIFICATE COURSE
Welcome to the TESOL International Certificate Course delivered and awarded by LTi Australia!

To complete your registration, please follow the two steps below:

A. Complete the Entry Test
This is part of the registration process. You are required to take an entry test that includes a multiple-choice quiz to assess your readiness for study. The test evaluates key skills such as language proficiency, technological literacy, numeracy, and logical reasoning.
• To start the entry test, please follow these steps:
  1. Visit https://tesolcourse.online/vietnam/login/signup.php and follow the instructions to create an account for the test.
    →Make sure that the name and email address you use match the information you will provide when registering for the course and receiving your certificate.
  2. After creating your account, log in to the system and select "TESOL Course Entry Test" to begin.
Note: You will have a maximum of 30 minutes to complete the test and must achieve at least 80% to meet the entry requirements and proceed with course registration. If you score below 80%, you may retake the test after 3 months.
B. Provide Proof of English Proficiency

Please log in to your account on the system, then select "Upload your English proficiency evidence" to upload a file containing your certificate, transcript, or diploma as proof of your English proficiency.

Minimum English Proficiency Requirements:
  • IELTS: 5.5
  • PTE Academic: 46
  • TOEIC: 700
  • CEFR: B1
  • TOEFL (Paper): 525
  • TOEFL (CBT): 196
  • TOEFL (IBT): 69-70
  • Cambridge Exams: FCE
  • ISLPR: 3
  • University Bachelor Degree in English Language

If you do not have an existing English proficiency certificate, you can take a free online English test at https://www.efset.org/4-skill/

Please note: The test takes about 90 minutes and evaluates your speaking, writing, listening, and reading skills. It can only be taken once and must be completed in one sitting within 90 minutes. After completing the test, you will receive a certificate of results, which you can save and upload to the "Upload your English proficiency evidence" section in the system.
